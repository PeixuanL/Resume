# HR 高频问题：薪酬、通勤、离职与简历追问

> 用途：根据你过往 HR 面常被问的问题，提前准备外籍 HR 面也适用的英文口径。
> 判断：这些问题在外籍 HR 面同样适用，只是表达方式更偏英文职业沟通，不一定像中文 HR 那样细问社保、底薪结构。

## 外籍 HR 面是否会问这些

会，而且很常见。外籍 HR 初面通常筛选：

1. 有没有相关产品经验。
2. 有没有 0-1 产品或完整产品生命周期经验。
3. 简历关键词是否真实，能不能讲清楚。
4. 离职原因、薪酬期望、到岗时间、通勤是否可接受。
5. 英文沟通是否顺畅，动机是否合理，候选人风险是否可控。

常见英文问法：

```text
Have you worked on a similar product before?
```

```text
Can you walk me through your 0-to-1 product experience?
```

```text
Can you tell me more about the AI / Agent product on your resume?
```

```text
Why are you considering leaving your current company?
```

```text
What is your salary expectation?
```

```text
What is your notice period?
```

```text
Are you okay with the location and commute?
```

## Q1. 有没有做过和 Trucker Path 方向类似的产品

不要硬说做过卡车运输产品。稳妥回答是：没有直接 trucking 经验，但做过相似复杂度的产品问题。

```text
I haven’t worked on a trucking product directly, but I have worked on products with similar complexity: location-based scenarios, real-time operational data, workflow optimization, and users making decisions based on data quality.
```

接 Trucker Path：

```text
For Trucker Path, I see the connection in route planning, real-time information, parking availability, weigh station status, and user-generated updates. These are different from my current industry, but the product thinking is transferable.
```

中文底层逻辑：

> 没做过卡车运输，但做过复杂真实场景、实时数据、工作流和数据质量影响决策的产品。

## Q2. 有没有做过从 0 到 1 的产品

可以讲 Inspector / AI 设施运维产品，控制在 1-2 分钟，不要讲太深。

```text
Yes. One representative example is an AI-enabled facility operations product I worked on from 0 to 1. The problem was that equipment, alarms, work orders, SOPs, and operational knowledge were scattered across different systems. We needed to build a more standardized product workflow instead of relying on highly customized delivery.
```

你的动作：

```text
I helped define the core product scope, including asset information, alarm management, work order workflow, knowledge base, and AI-assisted diagnosis. I worked on requirement analysis, PRD, prototype logic, cross-functional alignment, and delivery validation.
```

结果：

```text
The MVP covered most high-priority requirements from a benchmark client, supported several business opportunities, and helped shorten delivery cycles in similar projects.
```

如果 HR 追问你的角色：

```text
My role was mainly on the product side: understanding user scenarios, defining requirements, clarifying product boundaries, aligning with engineering and design, and validating whether the workflow could support real delivery.
```

## Q3. 简历上写 Agent / RAG / AI，请介绍一下

外籍 HR 不一定懂很深，讲产品机制，不讲模型训练。

```text
In my resume, Agent and RAG refer to AI product mechanisms in an operations workflow. The idea is not just to add a chatbot, but to help users understand an alarm, retrieve relevant equipment information or SOP knowledge, suggest possible causes, and generate a next-step action or work order draft.
```

边界句，必须记：

```text
I focused on product design, workflow, input-output boundaries, human confirmation, and fallback rules, not model training.
```

如果要连接 Trucker Path：

```text
The connection to Trucker Path is that both products rely on trustworthy information and clear decision support. For truck drivers, the product also needs to provide timely and reliable information so users can make better decisions on the road.
```

## Q4. 简历上写 70% 标品化，怎么解释

```text
The 70% is an estimated result based on reviewing repeated requirements across multiple customer projects. We grouped requirements into three layers: platform-level capabilities, industry common needs, and customer-specific customization.
```

```text
The reusable parts, such as asset structure, alarm workflow, work order status, and implementation checklist, were moved into the standard product baseline. Highly customer-specific integrations stayed as customization.
```

中文提醒：

> 不要说成严密财务数字。说是基于多项目需求盘点后的估算，重点在“把重复需求沉淀进产品基线”。

## Q5. 为什么离职 / 为什么看机会

不要说现公司不好。说下一阶段希望接触更大用户规模、更强反馈闭环和全球用户。

```text
I’m grateful for my current role because it gave me strong experience in product ownership, complex scenarios, and cross-functional delivery. At this stage, I’m looking for a role with a larger user base, stronger user feedback loop, and more exposure to global users. That’s why Trucker Path is interesting to me.
```

更短版本：

```text
I’m looking for a role where I can apply my structured product thinking to a larger user base and a more global product context.
```

## Q6. 薪酬期望

优先不要报死，先锚定岗位范围和整体 package。

```text
My expectation is generally aligned with the posted range for this role. I’m open to discussing the exact package based on the role scope, team expectations, and overall compensation structure.
```

如果必须说数字：

```text
Based on the posted range, I would expect something around 20 to 25K monthly, with the final number depending on the full package and role scope.
```

如果问当前薪资：

```text
I would prefer to focus on the expected range for this role and the overall fit. My expectation is aligned with the posted range, and I’m open to discussing details later in the process.
```

## Q7. 通勤 / 地点

```text
The location is acceptable for me. I would like to understand the working model, whether it is fully onsite or hybrid, but in general the commute is manageable.
```

如果实际通勤较远：

```text
It will take some time, but it is manageable for the right opportunity.
```

## Q8. 到岗时间 / notice period

```text
My notice period is negotiable. I would try to make the transition as smooth as possible.
```

如果你想表达可以尽快：

```text
I can discuss the timeline and try to join as soon as possible after completing the handover.
```

## Q9. 英文沟通是否 OK

```text
Yes, I’m comfortable working in English. I studied at UCL and have experience with English documents, presentations, and cross-cultural communication. I may not speak like a native speaker, but I can use English for product discussions, requirement clarification, and daily collaboration.
```

## 最优先背的 6 个问题

1. `Have you worked on a similar product before?`
2. `Can you walk me through your 0-to-1 product experience?`
3. `Can you explain the AI / Agent product on your resume?`
4. `Why are you leaving your current company?`
5. `What is your salary expectation?`
6. `Are you comfortable working in English?`

## 最后收束句

```text
Overall, I don’t want to overclaim direct trucking experience, but I do believe my experience in complex workflows, real-time data, product documentation, and cross-functional delivery is highly transferable to this role.
```
