# 外籍 HR 面试准备

> 目标：让 HR 觉得你英文沟通顺、动机合理、岗位理解到位、迁移能力成立、风险可控。

## 面试主线

中文版本：

> 我不是传统 C 端社区 PM，但我有复杂业务产品、用户工作流分析、PRD / 原型、跨团队交付和英文协作经验。Trucker Path 的核心不是泛娱乐社区，而是围绕北美卡车司机真实工作场景的导航、实时信息、UGC 和效率平台，所以我的方法论可以迁移，同时我也愿意补齐 C 端增长和社区机制。

英文版本：

```text
My background is not a traditional consumer social product background, but I have strong experience in complex workflow analysis, PRD and prototype design, cross-functional delivery, and English communication. I see Trucker Path as a product that solves real-world operational problems for truck drivers through location data, real-time information, and community contribution. That is why I think my experience is transferable, and I’m also excited to grow in consumer growth and UGC mechanisms.
```

## 60 秒自我介绍

```text
Hi, I’m Peixuan. I’m currently a B2B product manager at DataMesh, focusing on AI-enabled digital twin products for industrial and facility operations.

My core strength is turning complex real-world workflows into structured product solutions. I have worked across user research, requirement analysis, PRD and prototype design, cross-functional delivery, product launch, and iteration. In my current role, I work closely with R&D, design, testing, sales, and delivery teams.

Although my previous experience is more B2B and industrial, I think the transferable part is strong. Trucker Path also solves real-world operational problems: truck-safe routing, parking availability, fuel stops, weigh station status, load matching, and driver community. I’m very interested in products that combine user needs, location data, workflow efficiency, and industry-specific scenarios.
```

## 高频问题与推荐回答

### Q1. Why are you interested in this role?

```text
I’m interested in this role because Trucker Path is not just a navigation app. It combines truck-safe routing, parking, fuel stops, weigh station information, load matching, and driver community. I like products where user value is concrete and connected to real daily workflows.
```

### Q2. Why Trucker Path?

```text
What attracts me is the combination of location-based product design, real-time information, and community contribution. For truck drivers, accurate and fresh information can directly affect time, cost, safety, and income. I find that very meaningful from a product perspective.
```

### Q3. Your experience is more B2B. Why do you want a C-side product role?

```text
I don’t see this as a complete switch. The users are different, but the product thinking is connected. In my current role, I also need to understand user scenarios, break down workflows, define product requirements, work with design and engineering, and validate outcomes.

For this role, I would bring structured product thinking and cross-functional delivery experience. At the same time, I’m excited to grow in consumer product metrics, community engagement, and UGC mechanisms.
```

### Q4. What is your biggest strength?

```text
My biggest strength is structured product thinking. I’m good at taking a complex scenario, identifying key user pain points, breaking it down into product modules, and aligning different teams to deliver it.

I’m also comfortable working in English and in cross-functional environments, which I think is important for a global product like Trucker Path.
```

### Q5. What is one area you need to improve?

```text
The area I need to strengthen is direct consumer growth experience, such as retention, activation, and community contribution mechanisms. But I’m already learning this, and I think my experience in user workflow analysis and product delivery gives me a strong foundation to ramp up quickly.
```

### Q6. Are you comfortable working in English?

```text
Yes. I studied at UCL and have experience working with English documents, presentations, and cross-cultural communication. I may not speak like a native speaker, but I’m comfortable using English for product discussion, requirement clarification, and teamwork.
```

### Q7. Why are you considering leaving your current company?

```text
I’m grateful for my current experience because it gave me a strong foundation in product ownership, complex scenarios, and cross-functional delivery. At this stage, I’m looking for a role where I can work on a product with a larger user base, stronger user feedback loop, and more exposure to global users. Trucker Path is attractive to me because it combines practical user value, industry depth, and consumer-facing product challenges.
```

### Q8. Do you have App product experience?

```text
My previous work is not a pure consumer mobile app, but I have worked on product flows, user scenarios, prototypes, and front-end interaction logic. I’m familiar with defining user journeys, writing requirements, and working with design and engineering to deliver usable product experiences. For mobile consumer products, I would pay more attention to onboarding, activation, retention, and daily user engagement.
```

## 你可以反问 HR 的问题

```text
Which product area will this role focus on most: navigation, driver community, load board, or growth?
```

```text
What kind of product background does the team value most for this position?
```

```text
How does the Beijing product team collaborate with overseas colleagues and North American users?
```

```text
What would success look like for this role in the first six months?
```

## 不能踩的坑

1. 不要假装自己很懂美国卡车行业。
2. 不要把 AI 讲成唯一优势，岗位核心是 App / 社区 / 导航 / 货运场景。
3. 不要说“我想转 C 端”，要说“我的复杂场景产品方法可以迁移”。
4. 不要主动暴露年限焦虑，除非 HR 直接问。
5. 不要把缺少 UGC / 增长经验说成硬伤，要说成正在补齐的成长点。
