## 8. 不建议写法

避免以下表达，容易显得过度包装或与事实边界不一致：

- `AI algorithm Product Manager`
- `Led model training`
- `Full-stack AI engineer`
- `Owned the entire digital twin platform`
- `Expert in LLM architecture`
- 大量堆内部产品名：`Inspector / Designer / Director / DFS`

更好的写法是：

- `AI-enabled product workflows`
- `Agent-assisted operations`
- `Human-in-the-loop AI outputs`
- `Digital twin and industrial AI products`
- `B2B productization of customized customer scenarios`
